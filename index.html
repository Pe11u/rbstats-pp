Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how our browser extension collects, processes, and protects your data. We are fully committed to ensuring user privacy, safeguarding your information, and maintaining absolute transparency regarding our extension's operations.

1. Core Purpose of the Extension

The primary and single purpose of this browser extension is to provide a dedicated utility for Roblox users to manage, monitor, and switch seamlessly between their main account and sub-accounts (alt accounts) in real time. It centralizes and integrates the visualization of statistical data (such as RAP and Robux balances) and optimizes session switching for an efficient experience.

Strict Data Privacy Guarantee: We explicitly declare that we do not, and will never, collect or harvest any user data in any recognizable, hidden, or external format.

2. Data Collection and Processing

To fulfill its core utility locally on your browser, the extension requires access to specific technical parameters. This data is processed in a completely decentralized and private manner:

  • Roblox Authentication Tokens (Cookies): The extension securely reads and manages the .ROBLOSECURITY token. This is strictly required to enable direct, high-speed account switching (session swapping) directly inside the extension UI without forcing users to open incognito/secret windows. This data is entirely local and is never transmitted anywhere.
  • Local Account Information: The extension stores user-registered account names, avatar URLs, and fetched statistical parameters locally. This data remains completely restricted to the user's terminal environment and is never sent to external servers.

3. Permissions Required and Justification

To deliver its functionality safely and correctly, the extension requests the following web permissions:

Permission / API Operational Purpose & Justification
cookies Used to securely read and swap the .ROBLOSECURITY authentication cookie, allowing swift account switching directly within the extension UI.
storage Utilized to cache account metadata (usernames, avatar URLs, local stats) safely inside your browser's local sandbox environment.
tabs Required to automatically refresh active Roblox tabs immediately after an account switch, allowing the user to start using the new account seamlessly without manual reloads.

4. Host Permissions & Cross-Origin Communication

The extension requires cross-origin access to specific host domains to ensure core features work correctly. It strictly interfaces with the following endpoints:

  • https://*.roblox.com/: Connects to official Roblox APIs (Economy, Inventory, Users, Auth, etc.) to securely fetch real-time data for the logged-in user and linked sub-accounts—including RAP, Robux balance, User ID, and status. It is also required to validate active tokens and authenticate sessions during switching.
  • https://*.rbxcdn.com/: Connects to Roblox's Content Delivery Network (CDN) to securely retrieve and display official user avatar thumbnails, ensuring you can visually identify which account is active at a glance.

5. Third-Party Sharing and Tracking

The extension operates on a zero-tracking architecture. No analytic frameworks, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising scripts are embedded. Absolutely zero information is shared with, transmitted to, or stored by any third-party entity or external server. All data operations begin and end locally on your machine.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect technical adjustments or store standards. Any revisions will maintain our foundational commitment to absolute data isolation and zero external tracking.
